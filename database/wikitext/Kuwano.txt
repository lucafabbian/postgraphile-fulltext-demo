Kuwano (written: 桑野) is a Japanese surname. Notable people with the surname include:

Hijiri Kuwano (桑野 聖, born 1964), Japanese violinist, composer and arranger
Michiko Kuwano (桑野 通子, 1915–1946), Japanese actress
Nobuyoshi Kuwano (桑野 信義, born 1957), Japanese television performer
