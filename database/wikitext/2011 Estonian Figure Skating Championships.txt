The 2011 Estonian Figure Skating Championships (Estonian: Eesti Meistrivõistlused 2010) took place between 17 and 19 December 2010 in Narva. Skaters competed in the disciplines of men's singles, ladies' singles, and ice dancing on the senior and junior levels. The results were used to choose the teams to the 2011 World Championships and the 2011 European Championships.
