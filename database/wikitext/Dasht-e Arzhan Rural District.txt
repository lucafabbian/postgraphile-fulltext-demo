Dasht-e Arzhan Rural District (Persian: دهستان دشت ارژن) is a rural district (dehestan) in Arzhan District, Shiraz County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 4,901, in 1,089 families.  The rural district has 14 villages.
