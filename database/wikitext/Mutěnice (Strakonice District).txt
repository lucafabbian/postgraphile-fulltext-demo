Mutěnice is a municipality and village in Strakonice District in the South Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic. It has about 300 inhabitants.
Mutěnice lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Strakonice, 52 km (32 mi) north-west of České Budějovice, and 102 km (63 mi) south of Prague.
