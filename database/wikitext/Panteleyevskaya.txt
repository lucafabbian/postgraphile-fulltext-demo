Panteleyevskaya (Russian: Пантелеевская) is a rural locality (a village) in Yavengskoye Rural Settlement, Vozhegodsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 26 as of 2002.


== Geography ==
Panteleyevskaya is located 27 km northeast of Vozhega (the district's administrative centre) by road. Ulitinskaya is the nearest rural locality.
