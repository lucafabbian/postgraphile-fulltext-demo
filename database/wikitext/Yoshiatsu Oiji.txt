Yoshiatsu Oiji (生地 慶充, Oiji Yoshiatsu, born April 2, 1998) is a Japanese football player. He plays for FC Tokyo.


== Career ==
Yoshiatsu Oiji joined FC Tokyo in 2016. On March 13, he debuted in J3 League (v SC Sagamihara).
