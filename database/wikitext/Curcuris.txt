Curcuris (Sardinian: Crucùris) is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Oristano in the Italian region Sardinia, located about 60 kilometres (37 mi) northwest of Cagliari and about 25 kilometres (16 mi) southeast of Oristano.
Curcuris borders the following municipalities: Ales, Gonnosnò, Morgongiori, Pompu, Simala.
