Orelec [ɔˈrɛlɛt͡s] (Ukrainian: Орелець, Orelets’) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Olszanica, within Lesko County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) east of Lesko and 108 km (67 mi) south-east of the regional capital Rzeszów.The village has a population of 420.
