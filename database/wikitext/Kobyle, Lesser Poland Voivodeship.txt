Kobyle [kɔˈbɨlɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowy Wiśnicz, within Bochnia County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) east of Nowy Wiśnicz, 10 km (6 mi) south-east of Bochnia, and 45 km (28 mi) east of the regional capital Kraków.The village has a population of 1,250.
