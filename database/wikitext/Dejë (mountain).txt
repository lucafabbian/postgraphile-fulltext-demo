Dejë Mountain (Albanian: Mali i Dejës) is a mountain in northern Albania. Dejë Mountains highest peak is Maja e Dejës which reaches a height of 2,245 m (7,365 ft). Dejë Mountain is part of Lura-Dejes Mountain National Park since 2018. The nearest village is Macukull, in Dibër County.
