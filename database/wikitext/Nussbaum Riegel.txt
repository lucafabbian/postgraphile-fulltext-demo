Nussbaum Riegel (77°40′S 162°46′E) is a riegel or rock-bar across Taylor Valley in Victoria Land, extending from the vicinity of Sollas Glacier toward Lake Chad. Charted and named by the British Antarctic Expedition under Scott, 1910–13.
