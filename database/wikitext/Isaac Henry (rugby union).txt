Isaac Henry (born 8 March 1999) is an Australian rugby union player who plays for the Reds in Super Rugby. His playing position is centre. He was named in the Reds squad for the 2021 Super Rugby AU season. He previously represented the Brisbane City in the 2019 National Rugby Championship.
