Zawada [zaˈvada] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Poniec, within Gostyń County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) west of Poniec, 22 km (14 mi) south-west of Gostyń, and 73 km (45 mi) south of the regional capital Poznań.
