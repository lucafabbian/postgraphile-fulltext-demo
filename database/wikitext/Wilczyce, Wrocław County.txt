Wilczyce [vilˈt͡ʂɨt͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Długołęka, within Wrocław County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) south-west of Długołęka, and 9 kilometres (6 mi) east of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has a population of 590.
