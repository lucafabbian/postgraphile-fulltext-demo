Róża [ˈruʐa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Susiec, within Tomaszów Lubelski County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Susiec, 15 km (9 mi) west of Tomaszów Lubelski, and 97 km (60 mi) south-east of the regional capital Lublin.
