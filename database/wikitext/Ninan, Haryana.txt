Ninan is a village in the Bhiwani district of the Indian state of Haryana. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2.5 mi) east of the district headquarters town of Bhiwani. Jhajharia and Dhankar are the gotras of Jats in this village. As of the 2011 Census of India, the village had 293 households with a total population of 1,594 of which 836 were male and 758 female.
