Sadovaya (Russian: Садовая) is a rural locality (a village) in Verkh-Invenskoye Rural Settlement, Kudymkarsky District, Perm Krai, Russia. The population was 49 as of 2010. There is 1 street.


== Geography ==
Sadovaya is located 31 km southwest of Kudymkar (the district's administrative centre) by road. Antonova is the nearest rural locality.
