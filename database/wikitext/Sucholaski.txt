Sucholaski [suxɔˈlaski] (German: Sucholasken) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wydminy, within Giżycko County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland.It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-west of Wydminy, 15 km (9 mi) east of Giżycko, and 101 km (63 mi) east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
