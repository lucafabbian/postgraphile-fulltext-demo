Pyxine is a genus of foliose lichens in the family Caliciaceae. The genus has a widespread distribution in tropical regions.The presence or absence of the compound lichexanthone is a character used in classifying Pyxine  species; about 20 species contain this compound. This represents the largest group of foliose lichens with the compound.
