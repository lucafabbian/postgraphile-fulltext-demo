Karanje is a village in the Karmala taluka of Solapur district in Maharashtra state, India.


== Demographics ==
Covering 1,005 hectares (2,480 acres) and comprising 420 households at the time of the 2011 census of India, Karanje had a population of 1799. There were 958 males and 841 females, with 184 people being aged six or younger.
