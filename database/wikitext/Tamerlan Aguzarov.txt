Tamerlan Kimovich Aguzarov (Russian: Тамерлан Кимович Агузаров, Ossetian: Ӕгъуызарты Кимы фырт Тамерлан, romanized: Ahuezârte Kime fert Tâmêrlân; 14 June 1963 – 19 February 2016) was a Russian politician who was the head of North Ossetia-Alania from 2015 until his death in 2016.From 1999 to 2011 was Chairman of the Supreme Court of North Ossetia-Alania.
