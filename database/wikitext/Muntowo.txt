Muntowo [munˈtɔvɔ] (German: Muntowen; 1938–45: Muntau) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Mrągowo, within Mrągowo County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Mrągowo and 59 km (37 mi) east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
