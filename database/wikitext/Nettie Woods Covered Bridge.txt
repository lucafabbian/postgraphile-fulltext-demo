Nettie Woods Covered Bridge is a historic wooden covered bridge located at Center Township in Greene County, Pennsylvania. It is a 40-foot-long (12 m), Queenpost truss bridge with a tin covered gable roof, constructed in 1882.  It crosses Pursley Creek.  As of October 1978, it was one of nine historic covered bridges in Greene County.It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1979.
