Stepankovskaya (Russian: Степанковская) is a rural locality (a village) in Pakshengskoye Rural Settlement of Velsky District, Arkhangelsk Oblast, Russia. The population was 81 as of 2014. There is 1 street.


== Geography ==
Stepankovskaya is located 37 km north of Velsk (the district's administrative centre) by road. Yefremkovskaya is the nearest rural locality.
