Yuta Imamura (今村雄太, Imamura Yūta) (born 31 October 1984 in Mie, Japan) is a Japanese rugby union player. Imamura has played 38 international matches for the Japan national rugby union team.Imamura was a member of the Japan team at the 2007 Rugby World Cup and the 2011 Rugby World Cup.
