Adis Bećiragić (Turkish: Aziz Bekir; born 18 June 1969) is a Bosnian-Turkish professional basketball coach and former player. He was a backbone player on the Bosnian national basketball team in the 1990s. Bećiragić ended his playing career in 2000, and has worked as a basketball coach since 2000. He was head coach of Torku Konyaspor between 2013 and 2016, and was head coach for Uşak Sportif from 2017 to 2018.During his time playing in Turkey, Bećiragić took Turkish citizenship under the name Aziz Bekir.
