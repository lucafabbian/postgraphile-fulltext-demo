Dorunak (Persian: درونك, also Romanized as Dorūnak, Darūnak, and Derūnak; also known as Darinak and Qal‘eh-ye Dorūnak) is a village in Famur Rural District, Jereh and Baladeh District, Kazerun County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 359, in 69 families.
