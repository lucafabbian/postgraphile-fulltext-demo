Stung Treng municipality (Khmer: ក្រុងស្ទឹងត្រែង) is a municipality located in Stung Treng province, in north-east Cambodia. According to the 2019 census of Cambodia, it had a population of 37,103. The provincial capital Stung Treng town is located within the municipality.
