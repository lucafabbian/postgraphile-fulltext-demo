Pustkowo [pustˈkɔvɔ] (German: Pustchow) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Białogard, within Białogard County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 7 kilometres (4 mi) north of Białogard and 120 km (75 mi) north-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
