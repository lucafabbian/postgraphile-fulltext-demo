Ulukent is a railway station in Ulukent, Izmir Province, Turkey. The station is served by İZBAN's Northern Line and TCDD's Alsancak-Uşak Express. The station is 24.44 km (15.19 mi) away from Alsancak Terminal. For the construction of the Karşıyaka Tunnel, all trains started and terminated at Ulukent between 2008 and 2010.
