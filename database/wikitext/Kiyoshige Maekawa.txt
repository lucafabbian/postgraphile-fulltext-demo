Kiyoshige Maekawa (前川 清成, Maekawa Kiyoshige, born December 22, 1962) is a Japanese politician of the Democratic Party of Japan, a member of the House of Councillors in the Diet (national legislature).


== Overview ==

A native of Kashihara, Nara and graduate of Kansai University, he was elected for the first time in 2004.
