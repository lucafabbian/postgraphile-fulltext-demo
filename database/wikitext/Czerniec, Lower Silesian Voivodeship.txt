Czerniec [ˈt͡ʂɛrɲɛt͡s] (German: Schwarzau) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lubin, within Lubin County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland.It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) east of Lubin, and 59 kilometres (37 mi) north-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The village has a population of 290.
