Cendrim Kameraj (definite Albanian form: Çëndrim Kameraj), also known as Qëndrim Kameraj (born 13 March 1999) is a professional footballer who plays for Kosovan club Dukagjini. Born in Switzerland, Kameraj represents Kosovo internationally.


== Club career ==
He made his Serie C debut for Juventus U23 on 27 September 2018 in a game against Cuneo.On 26 January 2019, Kameraj joined Swiss club Lugano until 30 June 2021.On 27 January 2022, Kameraj signed with Kriens.On 10 August 2022, Kameraj signed with Dukagjini in Kosovo.


== International career ==
Kameraj was born in Switzerland and is of Kosovan descent. Kameraj represented Switzerland as a youth international, before switching to represent Kosovo in 2019.
