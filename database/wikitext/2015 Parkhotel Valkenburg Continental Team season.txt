The 2015 women's road cycling season was the second for the Parkhotel Valkenburg Continental Team as a UCI women's team and the third year for the team which began as Parkhotel Valkenburg p/b Math Salden in 2013.

== UCI World Ranking ==

The 2015 UCI Women's Road Rankings are rankings based upon the results in all UCI-sanctioned races of the 2015 women's road cycling season.
Parkhotel Valkenburg finished 20th in the 2015 ranking for UCI teams.
