Wey (Chinese: 魏派; pinyin: Wèipài, pronunciation: 喂 wéi, stylised as WEY) is an automotive marque owned by the Chinese automaker Great Wall Motors. Launched in 2016, the brand focused on premium crossovers and SUVs based on Haval models.


== History ==
The name Wey is derived from Great Wall Motor chairman Wei Jianjun's surname, which is pronounced the same way.
