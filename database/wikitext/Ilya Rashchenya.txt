Ilya Vasilyevich Rashchenya (Belarusian: Ілля Васільевіч Рашчэня; Russian: Илья Васильевич Ращеня; born 27 May 1997) is a Belarusian professional footballer who plays for Russian club Volgar Astrakhan.
