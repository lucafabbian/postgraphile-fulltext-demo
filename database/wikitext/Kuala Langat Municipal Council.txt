Kuala Langat Municipal Council (Malay: Majlis Perbandaran Kuala Langat) is the municipal council which administers Kuala Langat District. This agency is under the purview of Selangor state government.


== History ==
Established on January 1, 1977, under the Local Government Act 1976 by a combination of 3 Local Councils: Sg. Jarom, Tanjung Sepat Local Council, Kuala Langat Municipal Board.Early establishment 3.7 square miles (9.6 km), 2001 increased to 62,294 km2 and 2006 increased to 857.70 km2 including 3 nautical miles.
