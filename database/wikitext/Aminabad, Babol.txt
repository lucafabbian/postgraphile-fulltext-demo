Aminabad (Persian: امین‌آباد, also Romanized as Amīnābād) is a village in Karipey Rural District of Lalehabad District, Babol County, Mazandaran province, Iran.
At the 2006 National Census, its population was 1,970 in 550 households. The following census in 2011 counted 2,019 people in 639 households. The latest census in 2016 showed a population of 1,952 people in 672 households; it was the largest village in its rural district.
