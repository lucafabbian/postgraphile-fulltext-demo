Tursko [ˈturskɔ] (German: Turzig, Kashubian: Tërskò) is a village in Gmina Miastko, Bytów County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 21 kilometres (13 mi) west of Bytów and 70 km (43 mi) south-west of Gdańsk (capital city of the Pomeranian Voivodeship). 
From 1975 to 1998 the village was in Słupsk Voivodeship. 
It has a population of 199.
