Dianbu (Chinese: 店埠; pinyin: Diànbù) is a town in and the seat of Feidong County in the eastern suburbs of Hefei, the capital of Anhui province, People's Republic of China. As of 2011, it has 14 residential communities (社区) and 16 villages under its administration.
