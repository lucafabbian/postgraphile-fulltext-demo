Gilbira (Russian: Гильбира; Buryat: Гэльбэрэ, Gelbere) is a rural locality (an ulus) in Ivolginsky District, Republic of Buryatia, Russia. The population was 93 as of 2010. There are 10 streets.


== Geography ==
Gilbira is located 37 km southwest of Ivolginsk (the district's administrative centre) by road. Kokorino is the nearest rural locality.
