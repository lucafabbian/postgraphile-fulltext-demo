The 1918–19 County Antrim Shield was the 30th edition of the County Antrim Shield, a cup competition in Irish football.Distillery won the tournament for the 9th time, defeating Belfast Celtic 1–0 in the final replay at Windsor Park, after the original final at The Oval finished 0–0.
