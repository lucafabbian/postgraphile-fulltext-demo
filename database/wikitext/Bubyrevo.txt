Bubyrevo (Russian: Бубырево) is a rural locality (a village) in Novlenskoye Rural Settlement, Vologodsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 3 as of 2002.


== Geography ==
Bubyrevo is located 71 km northwest of Vologda (the district's administrative centre) by road. Isakovo is the nearest rural locality.
