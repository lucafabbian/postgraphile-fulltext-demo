Wola Korzeniowa [ˈvɔla kɔʐɛˈɲɔva] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Szydłowiec, within Szydłowiec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Szydłowiec and 114 km (71 mi) south of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 473.
