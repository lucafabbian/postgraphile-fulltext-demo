Nikola Gluščević (born 11 June 2001) is a Montenegrin footballer. He plays for Serbian club Budućnost Dobanovci.


== Career ==
In 2018, Gluščević signed for Spanish La Liga side Sevilla, where his father, Igor, played.For the second half of 2019–20, he was sent on loan to OFK Titograd in Montenegro.
In 2020, he signed for Serbian top flight club Proleter Novi Sad.
