Jacopo Sciamanna (born 24 May 1990) is an Italian footballer who currently plays as a striker for Monterosi.


== Club career ==
Having come through the youth system of Lazio, Sciamanna was sent on loan to San Marino Calcio in 2010 without having played a senior game for his parent club.On 24 August 2018, he joined Cavese.On 13 July 2019 he signed with Serie D side Monterosi.
