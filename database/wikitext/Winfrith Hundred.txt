Winfrith Hundred was a hundred in the county of Dorset, England, containing the following parishes:

Coombe Keynes
East Lulworth
East Stoke
Moreton (part)
Owermoigne (later a separate liberty)
Poxwell
Warmwell
Watercombe (from 1858)
Winfrith Newburgh
Woodsford
