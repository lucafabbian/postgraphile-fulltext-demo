Briceland is an unincorporated community in Humboldt County, California. It is 6 miles (10 km) west of Redway, at an elevation of 594 feet (181 m).A post office operated at Briceland from 1889 to 1968. The name honors John C. Briceland, who purchased the site in 1889.
