Kożuszki-Ośrodek [kɔˈʐuʂki ɔˈɕrɔdɛk] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Sochaczew, within Sochaczew County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) east of Sochaczew and 47 km (29 mi) west of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 330.
