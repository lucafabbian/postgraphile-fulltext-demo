Trinity Methodist Church, Castleford is located in Castleford, Wakefield District, West Yorkshire, England.The church is part of the Aire and Calder Methodist Circuit in the Yorkshire West District.The current Johannus Sweelinck 30 3-manual digital organ was installed in the church in the year 2000. The original organ was built by Nelson & Co., based in Durham.The church has been re-roofed. The Castleford Male Voice Choir meet in the church hall next to the church on a weekly basis.
