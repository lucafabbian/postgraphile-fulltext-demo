Milikowice [milikɔˈvit͡sɛ] (German: Arnsdorf) is a village in the urban-rural Gmina Jaworzyna Śląska, within Świdnica County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) south of Jaworzyna Śląska, 5 km (3 mi) north of Świdnica, and 52 km (32 mi) south-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
The early Gothic Parish Church of St. Michael the Archangel was built in the village in the mid-13th century and rebuilt in the 15th century.
