Stackhousia dielsii, commonly known as yellow stackhousia, is a species of plant in the family Celastraceae.The dense perennial herb typically grows to a height of 0.2 to 0.6 metres (0.7 to 2.0 ft) and has a sedge-like habit. It blooms between July and November and produces yellow-green flowers.The species is found on sandy soils in coastal areas of the Mid West region of Western Australia.
