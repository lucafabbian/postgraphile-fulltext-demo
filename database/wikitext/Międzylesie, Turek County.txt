Międzylesie [mjɛnd͡zɨˈlɛɕɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Władysławów, within Turek County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-east of Władysławów, 8 km (5 mi) north of Turek, and 113 km (70 mi) east of the regional capital Poznań.
