Keng-Suu Game Reserve (Kyrgyz: Кең-Суу зоологиялык (аң уулоочу) заказниги) is a specially protected area located in Tüp District of Issyk-Kul Region of Kyrgyzstan. It was established in 1990 to conserve and reproduce game animals including those listed in the Red Book.  The game reserve's area is 8,712 hectares.
