Li Kai-jie (Chinese: 李凱傑; born 22 July 1996) is a Taiwanese international footballer who plays as a forward for Taichung Futuro.


== International career ==
Li made his international debut in a 2–1 victory over Timor-Leste, playing 90 minutes.
