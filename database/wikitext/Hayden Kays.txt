Hayden Kays (born 1985 in London) is a British artist. He is influenced by the pop art from the '50s, but has most ties with street art.
He often uses a typewriter (font) in his artworks.  He has sold art to celebrities such as Chris Martin, Harry Styles and Noel Fielding.His works were collected in a monograph book in 2013, named Hayden Kays Is An Artist. (ISBN 978-0992726706)
In 2014, he made the cover art for rock bands The Kooks  and the Tribes (band).Between 2012 and 2014 Kays illustrated an anecdotal column for London’s Ham&High, written by journalist Adam Sonin, based on English Heritage’s Blue Plaque scheme, called Behind Closed Doors.
