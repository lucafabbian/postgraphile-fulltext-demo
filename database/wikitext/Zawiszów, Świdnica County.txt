Zawiszów [zaˈviʂuf] (German: Säbischdorf) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Świdnica, within Świdnica County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Świdnica, and 49 kilometres (30 mi) south-west of the regional capital Wrocław.
