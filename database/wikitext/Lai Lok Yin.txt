Lai Lok Yin (Chinese: 黎洛賢; born 20 July 1995 in Hong Kong) is a former Hong Kong professional footballer who played as a winger. 


== Club career ==
In 2015, Lai joined Dreams Metro Gallery.On 5 December 2015, Lai scored his first goal for Metro Gallery against Wong Tai Sin in a 2–0 victory.Following the announcement of Metro Gallery's hiatus, Lai became a free agent. He joined Pegasus at the conclusion of his contract on 30 June 2016. On 11 June 2017, HK Pegasus chairperson Canny Leung revealed that Lai would be leaving the club.He was not out of work for long as two weeks later, Lai signed with Rangers.
