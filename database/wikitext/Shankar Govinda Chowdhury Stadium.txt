Shankar Gobinda Choudhury Stadium (Bengali: শংকর গোবিন্দ চৌধুরী স্টেডিয়াম)is the district stadium of, Natore, Bangladesh. The stadium is located by the Children's Park of Natore municipality. Stadium is a multi-purpose venue, mainly used for national day parade, district level football completions.


== Hosted events ==
The zonal host of 4th National Football Championship from June 25 to July 1 in 2004.
The zonal host of 5th National Football Championship from September 15–26 in 2005.
