Iulius Mall Cluj is a shopping mall in Cluj-Napoca, Romania, and was opened on 10 November 2007.
It has 250 stores including one hypermarket Auchan (16,000 m2 (170,000 sq ft)) and several anchors, such as : Auchan, Inditex Group (Zara, Bershka, Pull & Bear, Stradivarius, Massimo Dutti), Tommy Hilfiger, H&M, Cinema City.
It has a 10 screen cinema complex, 25 fast-food and three restaurants.
It has a 2,000 m2 (22,000 sq ft) swimming pool and a fitness club.
There are 2,180 parking spaces.
It also has a 10,000 m2 (110,000 sq ft) exterior park with a lake view, called Iulius Park.
