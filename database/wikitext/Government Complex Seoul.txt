The Government Complex Seoul (Korean: 정부서울청사), formerly known as Central Government Complex (building) (Korean: 정부중앙청사) is government office building complex in Jongno, Seoul. It has one main building and two annex building. In year 2022, the Complex is used by several Ministries of South Korea, including Financial Services Commission, Ministry of the Interior and Safety and Ministry of Unification.
