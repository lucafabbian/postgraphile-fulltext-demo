This is a list of tallest buildings in Estonia. All buildings over 50 m (164 ft) are listed. Only habitable building are ranked, which excludes radio masts and towers, observation towers, steeples, chimneys and other tall architectural structures. For those, see List of tallest structures in Estonia.
