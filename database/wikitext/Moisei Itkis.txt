Moisei  Abramovich Itkis (Russian: Моисей Абрамович Иткис; 20 April 1929 – 22 June 2009) was a Soviet sports shooter. He competed in the 300 metre rifle, three positions event at the 1960 Summer Olympics.19-time record holder world and Europe. 75-time record holder of the USSR.  He was awarded the Order of the Badge of Honour.Graduated from  Lesgaft National State University of Physical Education, Sport and Health. He taught at the Mozhaysky St. Petersburg Military Engineering Institute.
