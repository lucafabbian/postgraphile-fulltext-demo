Kohfidisch (German pronunciation: [koːˈfiːdɪʃ] (listen); Hungarian: Gyepűfüzes, Croatian: Gornji Fideš, Burgenland Croatian: Zgornji Fideš) is a municipality in the district of Oberwart in the Austrian state of Burgenland.


== Geography ==
The municipality is composed of the main town Kohfidisch and the two villages Kirchfidisch (German pronunciation: [ˌkɪʁçˈfiːdɪʃ] (listen)) and Harmisch.
