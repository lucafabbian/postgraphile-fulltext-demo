Kowalewice Nowe [kɔvalɛˈvit͡sɛ ˈnɔvɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Świercze, within Pułtusk County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) east of Świercze, 18 km (11 mi) west of Pułtusk, and 51 km (32 mi) north of Warsaw.
