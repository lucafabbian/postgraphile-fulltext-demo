Hüseyin Doğan (born 22 January 1994) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays as a midfielder.


== Club career ==
He came through the Sparta Rotterdam youth system and made his senior debut for them in September 2012 against Go Ahead Eagles.Without a club for a year after Sparta released him in 2016, Doğan signed with FC Oss ahead of the 2017–18 season.On 29 July 2019, Doğan signed for NAC Breda.On 29 March 2021, Doğan signed for FC Istiklol on a one-year contract following a trial with the club. On 7 June 2021, Istiklol announced that Doğan had left the club by mutual consent.On 22 September 2021, Doğan returned to TOP Oss.


== Personal life ==
Born in the Netherlands, Doğan is of Turkish descent.
