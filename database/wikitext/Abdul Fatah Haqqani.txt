Abdul Fatah Haqqani (Pashto: عبدالفتاح حقاني; died April 19, 2011) was a citizen of Afghanistan who was held in the Bagram Internment Facility, and was subsequently killed by US forces.He was killed on April 19, 2011, by a precision air strike in Burkah district, Baghlan Province.
The US claimed he commanded both Taliban forces, and elements of the Islamic Movement of Uzbekistan.
