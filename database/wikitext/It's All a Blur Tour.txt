It's All a Blur Tour is a co-headlining tour by Canadian rapper Drake, and American hip hop artist 21 Savage to promote their collaborative album Her Loss. It set to begin on June 29, 2023, in Memphis and end in Toronto, Canada on October 7, 2023. The tour will consist of 56 dates across United States and Canada. It will be Drake first tour in North America in five years.
