Mohamed Al Ghanodi (Arabic: محمد الغنودي; born 22 November 1992) is a Libyan international footballer who plays for Al-Ahli as a striker. He is a member of Libya national football team, played a match against Senegal in 2012 Africa Cup of Nations.
