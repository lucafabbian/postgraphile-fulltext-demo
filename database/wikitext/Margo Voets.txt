Margo Voets (born (1995-09-10)10 September 1995) is a Belgian female volleyball player. She is part of the Belgium women's national volleyball team and plays as libero.She competed at the 2017 FIVB Volleyball World Grand Prix. 
On club level she plays for VC Oudegem.
