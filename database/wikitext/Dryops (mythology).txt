In Greek mythology, Dryops (/ˈdraɪ.ɒps/, Ancient Greek: Δρύοψ, "oak-face", "wood-face" or "wood-eater") 

Dryops, a king of Oeta.
Dryops, a Trojan prince as one of the children of King Priam of Troy. He was killed by Achilles during the Trojan War.
Dryops, a companion of Aeneas killed by Clausus, an ally of Turnus, the man who opposed Aeneas in Italy.
