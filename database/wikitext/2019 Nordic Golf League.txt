The 2019 Nordic Golf League was the 21st season of the Nordic Golf League, one of four third-tier tours recognised by the European Tour.

== Order of Merit ==
The Order of Merit was titled as the GolfBox Road to Europe and was based on prize money won during the season, calculated using a points-based system. The top five players on the tour (not otherwise exempt) earned status to play on the 2020 Challenge Tour.
