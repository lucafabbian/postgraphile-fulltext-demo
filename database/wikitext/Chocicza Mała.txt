Chocicza Mała [xɔˈt͡ɕit͡ʂa ˈmawa] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Września, within Września County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south-west of Września and 45 km (28 mi) east of the regional capital Poznań.
