KGLC (100.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to Miami, Oklahoma, United States. The station is currently owned by Mark Linn, through licensee Taylor Made Broadcasting Network, LLC.  The station airs an adult contemporary format.


== History ==
This station was assigned call sign KGLC on March 7, 1991. KORS on January 1, 1979 and KSSM on November 25, 1981.  Their slogan is "Radio on the 
Route".
