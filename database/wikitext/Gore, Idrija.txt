Gore (pronounced [ˈɡoːɾɛ], in order sources Gora, German: Tschudenberg) is a settlement in the hills east of Idrija in the traditional Inner Carniola region of Slovenia.


== Church ==

The parish church in the settlement is dedicated to Mary Magdalene and belongs to the Koper Diocese.
