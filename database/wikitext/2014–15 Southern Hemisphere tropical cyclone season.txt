The 2014–15 Southern Hemisphere tropical cyclone season may refer to one of three different basins and respective seasons:

2014-15 South-West Indian Ocean cyclone season,  west of 90°E
2014-15 Australian region cyclone season, between 90°E and 160°E
2014-15 South Pacific cyclone season, east of 160°ETwo South Atlantic tropical cyclones formed during this time period, Subtropical Storm Bapo and Subtropical Storm Cari.
