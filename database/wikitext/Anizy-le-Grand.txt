Anizy-le-Grand (French pronunciation: ​[anizi lə ɡʁɑ̃]) is a commune in the department of Aisne in the Hauts-de-France region of northern France. It was established on 1 January 2019 from the merger of the communes of Anizy-le-Château, Faucoucourt and Lizy.
