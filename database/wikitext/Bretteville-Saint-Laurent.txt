Bretteville-Saint-Laurent (French pronunciation: ​[bʁɛtvil sɛ̃ loʁɑ̃]) is a commune in the Seine-Maritime department in the Normandy region in northern France.


== Geography ==
A small farming village situated in the Pays de Caux some 19 miles (31 km) southwest of Dieppe, at the junction of the D107, D142 and the D103 roads.
