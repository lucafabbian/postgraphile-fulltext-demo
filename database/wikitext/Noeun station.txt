Noeun Station (Korean: 노은역; Hanja: 老隱驛) is a station of Daejeon Metro Line 1 in Noeun-dong, Yuseong District, Daejeon, South Korea.


== Surroundings ==
In the vicinity of Nogun Station, there are Ji Jok Mountain, DaeJeon Ji jok High School, DaeJeon Ji juk Middle School, DaeJon SangJi Elementary School, Durubong Neighborhood Park and Eung- GuBi Park.The location is 161 underground of Noeun in Yuseong-gu, Daejeon Metropolitan City.
