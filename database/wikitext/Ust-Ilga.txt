Ust-Ilga (Russian: Усть-Илга) is a rural locality in Zhigalovsky District of Irkutsk Oblast, Russia. Population:   220 (2002 Census);


== Geography ==
The village is about 23 km (14 mi) north of Zhigalovo, the district administrative center. It lies on the left bank of the Ilga river near its confluence with the Lena.
