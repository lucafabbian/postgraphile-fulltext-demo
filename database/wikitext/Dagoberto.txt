Dagoberto Pelentier (born 22 March 1983) simply known as Dagoberto, is a Brazilian retired professional footballer who played as a forward.


== Career ==
Born in Dois Vizinhos, he formerly played for Atlético-PR, São Paulo and Internacional. He signed for Cruzeiro in January 2013.On 3 March 2015 Dagoberto was loaned to Vasco da Gama until the end of the 2015 season.On 29 June 2017, it was announced that Dagoberto signed with the San Francisco Deltas for the remainder of the 2017 NASL season.On 10 September 2019 he announced that he had retired from football at the age of 36.


== International career ==
Dagoberto has represented Brazil at Under-20 and Under-23 levels. He was a member of Brazil U-20 squad which won the FIFA World Youth Championship in 2003.
