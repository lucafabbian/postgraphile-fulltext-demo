Agheshlu (Persian: اغشلو, also Romanized as Āgheshlū and Agheshloo; also known as  Āgheshlī, Akishli, Akyshly, Āq Ashlū, and Āqeshlū) is a village in Valdian Rural District, Ivughli District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 673, in 129 families.
