Możdżanowo [mɔʐd͡ʐaˈnɔvɔ] (German: Mützenow) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Ustka, within Słupsk County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) south-west of Ustka, 17 km (11 mi) west of Słupsk, and 122 km (76 mi) west of the regional capital Gdańsk.
For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 220.
