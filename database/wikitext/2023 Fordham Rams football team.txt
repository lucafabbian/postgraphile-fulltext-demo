The 2023 Fordham Rams football team will represent Fordham University  as a member of the Patriot League during the 2023 NCAA Division I FCS football season. They are led by sixth-year head coach Joe Conlin and play their home games at Coffey Field.


== Previous season ==

The Rams finished the 2022 season with a record of 9–3, 5–1 Patriot League play to finish in second place. They lost to New Hampshire 52–42 in the NCAA Division I First Round.
