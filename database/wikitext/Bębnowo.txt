Bębnowo [bɛmbˈnɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Radzanów, within Mława County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south of Radzanów, 29 km (18 mi) south-west of Mława, and 99 km (62 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 140.
