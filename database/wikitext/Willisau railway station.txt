Willisau railway station (German: Bahnhof Willisau) is a railway station in the municipality of Willisau, in the Swiss canton of Lucerne. It is an intermediate stop on the standard gauge Huttwil–Wolhusen line of BLS AG.


== Services ==
The following services stop at Willisau:
Lucerne S-Bahn:
S6/S7: half-hourly service to Lucerne and greater than hourly service to Langenthal; all S6 and some S7 trains continue to Langenthal. S7 trains operate combined with a RegioExpress between Wolhusen and Lucerne.
S77: rush-hour service to Lucerne.
