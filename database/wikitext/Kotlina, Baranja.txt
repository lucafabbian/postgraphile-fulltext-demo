Kotlina (Hungarian: Sepse; Serbian Cyrillic: Koтлина) is a settlement in the region of Baranja, Croatia. Administratively, it is located in the Kneževi Vinogradi municipality within the Osijek-Baranja County. The population is 334 people.


== Ethnic groups (2001 census) ==
Hungarians = 295
Croats = 26
Serbs = 3
others = 10
