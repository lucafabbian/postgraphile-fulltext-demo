Alexander Mühling (né Bieler; born 5 September 1992) is a German professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for 2. Bundesliga club Holstein Kiel. He was born Alexander Bieler but decided to adopt his wife's surname, Mühling, in the summer of 2017.He was a youth international for Germany, earning three caps for the under-18 team in 2010.
