Kamlesh Shah is an Indian politician and a member of the Indian National Congress party.


== Career ==


== Political career ==
He became an MLA in 2013.He is the MLA of Amarwara (Vidhan Sabha constituency) and visits the villages and interacts with villages about the development works carried out in this region.


== Political views ==
He supports Congress Party's Ideology.


== Personal life ==
He is married to Mrs. Madhvi Shah.
