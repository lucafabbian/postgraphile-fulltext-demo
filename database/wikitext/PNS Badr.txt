PNS Badr may refer to following ships of Pakistan Navy:

PNS Badr (1957), the former British Battle-class destroyer HMS Gabbard (D47) acquired by Pakistan in 1957 and scrapped in 1985.
PNS Badr (1989), the former United States Brooke-class frigate USS Julius A. Furer (FFG-6) acquired by Pakistan in 1989 and returned in 1993. She was scrapped in 1994.
PNS Badr (1994), the former British Type 21 frigate HMS Alacrity (F174) acquired by Pakistan in 1994.
