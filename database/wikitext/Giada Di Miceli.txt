Giada Di Miceli (born 18 January 1987 in Rome) is an Italian television presenter and radio personality.


== Biography ==
Graduated in philosophy; from 2011 led the program  Non succederà più on Radio Manà Manà.Since 2014, she moved on Radio Radio Station (FM 104.5)
