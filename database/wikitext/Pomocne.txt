Pomocne [pɔˈmɔt͡snɛ] (German: Pombsen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Męcinka, within Jawor County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.
It lies approximately 11 km (7 mi) west of Jawor, and 71 km (44 mi) west of the regional capital Wrocław.
