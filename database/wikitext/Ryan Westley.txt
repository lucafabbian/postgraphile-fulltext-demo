Ryan Westley (born 19 August 1993) is a British slalom canoeist who has competed at the international level since 2009.He won four medals at the World Championships with two silvers (C1: 2018, C1 team: 2017) and two bronzes (C1: 2015, C1 team: 2018). He also won one gold and two bronze medals at the European Championships.
