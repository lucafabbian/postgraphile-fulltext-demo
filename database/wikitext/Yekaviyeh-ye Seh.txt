Yekaviyeh-ye Seh (Persian: يكاويه سه, also Romanized as Yekāvīyeh-ye Seh; also known as Lekāvīgeh-e Seh and Lekāvīgeh-ye Seh) is a village in Anaqcheh Rural District, in the Central District of Ahvaz County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 175, in 33 families.
