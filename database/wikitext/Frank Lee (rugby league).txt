Frank Lee (4 Oct 1880 - 1949) was an English professional rugby league footballer who played in the 1900s and 1910s. He played at representative level for England and Lancashire, and at club level for St. Helens and York, as a fullback, i.e. number 1, or, as a forward (prior to the specialist positions of; prop, hooker, second-row, loose forward), during the era of contested scrums.


== International honours ==
Frank Lee won a cap for England while at St. Helens in 1906 against Other Nationalities.
