Nowa Wieś [ˈnɔva ˈvjɛɕ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Kuczbork-Osada, within Żuromin County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) north-east of Żuromin and 121 km (75 mi) north-west of Warsaw.
The village has a population of 300.
