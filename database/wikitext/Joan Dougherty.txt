== Biography ==
Born in Montreal, Quebec, to Edward Mason, a physician, and Loretta O'Reilly, Dougherty studied at The Study, a private girls' school, and received a Bachelor of Science degree in 1947 and a Masters in histology in 1950 from McGill University. She did post-graduate studies at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in biophysics.Dougherty represented Jacques-Cartier in the National Assembly of Quebec from 1981 to 1987.She died from COVID-19 during the COVID-19 pandemic in Quebec.
