Bžany (Rusyn: Бжаны) is a village and municipality in Stropkov District in the Prešov Region of north-eastern Slovakia.


== History ==
In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1410.


== Geography ==
The municipality lies at an altitude of 170 metres and covers an area of 17.064 km². It has a population of about 172 people.


== Genealogical resources ==
