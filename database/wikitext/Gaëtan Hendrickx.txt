Gaëtan Hendrickx (born 30 March 1995) is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Deinze.He is the younger brother of fellow professional footballer Jonathan Hendrickx.


== Club career ==
On 14 August 2021, he signed a contract with Deinze for the term of two years with an option for the third.
