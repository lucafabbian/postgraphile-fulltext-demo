Ovtsyno (Russian: Овцыно) is a rural locality (a village) in Golovinskoye Rural Settlement, Sudogodsky District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. The population was 109 as of 2010. There are 4 streets.


== Geography ==
Ovtsyno is located on the Soyma River, 15 km northwest of Sudogda (the district's administrative centre) by road. Khokhlachi is the nearest rural locality.
