Páramo is a district of the Pérez Zeledón canton, in the San José province of Costa Rica.


== History ==
Páramo was created on 18 January 1984 by Acuerdo Ejecutivo 129.


== Geography ==
Páramo has an area of 203.17 km² and an elevation of 900 metres.


== Demographics ==
For the 2011 census, Páramo had a population of 4,410 inhabitants.
