The Altun-Alem Mosque (Serbian: Алтун-алем џамија, litreally 'Golden Gem Mosque') is a mosque located in Novi Pazar, Serbia.Abdul Gani, an architect, constructed the Altun-Alem Mosque in the first half of the 16th century.
