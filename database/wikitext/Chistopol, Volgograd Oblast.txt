Chistopol (Russian: Чистополь) is a rural locality (a khutor) in Grishinskoye Rural Settlement, Kikvidzensky District, Volgograd Oblast, Russia. The population was 132 as of 2010. There are 2 streets.


== Geography ==
Chistopol is located 22 km southeast of Preobrazhenskaya (the district's administrative centre) by road. Krutoy Log is the nearest rural locality.
