Grič pri Trebnjem (pronounced [ˈɡɾiːtʃ pɾi ˈtɾeːbnjɛm]) is a small settlement west of Trebnje in eastern Slovenia. It lies below the northern slopes of Fat Hill (Slovene: Debeli hrib, 420 m). The Municipality of Trebnje is part of the historical region of Lower Carniola and is now included in the Southeast Slovenia Statistical Region.


== Name ==
The name of the settlement was changed from Grič to Grič pri Trebnjem in 1955.
