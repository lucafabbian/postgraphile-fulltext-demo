Twin Pinnacles (62°8′S 58°6′W) is a rock 20 m (66 ft) high marked by two summits, lying 0.1 nautical miles (0.19 km; 0.12 mi) northeast of Lions Rump at the west side of the entrance to King George Bay in the South Shetland Islands. Charted and named during 1937 by DI personnel on the Discovery II.
