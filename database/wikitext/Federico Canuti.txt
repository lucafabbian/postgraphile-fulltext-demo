Federico Canuti (born 30 August 1985) is an Italian former professional road bicycle racer, who rode professionally between 2008 and 2013 for the Colnago–CSF Inox and Cannondale teams, and in 2017 for the D'Amico Utensilnord team.
Canuti was named a member of the Liquigas–Cannondale team for the 2012 Tour de France, making his début in the race.
After retiring from professional cycling, Canuti set up a cycling apparel brand, Kaiser KR Clothing.
