Haitian Center Tower 2 (Chinese: 海天中心2号楼) is a skyscraper that architecturally topped out in 2020. Located in the Shinan District of Qingdao, China, it is the tallest building in Shandong Province, among the 50 tallest buildings in China, and among the 100 tallest buildings in the world.
