Gevhande Khadak is a village in India, situated in Mawal taluka of Pune district in the state of Maharashtra. It encompasses an area of 285 ha (704 acres).


== Administration ==
The village is administrated by a sarpanch, an elected representative who leads a gram panchayat. At the time of the 2011 Census of India, the gram panchayat governed two villages and was based at Thakursai.


== Demographics ==
At the 2011 census, the village comprised 42 households. The population of 279 was split between 130 males and 149 females.


== Air travel connectivity ==
The closest airport to the village is Pune Airport.
