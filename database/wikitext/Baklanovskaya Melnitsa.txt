Baklanovskaya Melnitsa (Russian: Баклановская Мельница) is a rural locality (a village) in Gorodetskoye Rural Settlement, Kichmengsko-Gorodetsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 4 as of 2002.


== Geography ==
Baklanovskaya Melnitsa is located 47 km southwest of Kichmengsky Gorodok (the district's administrative centre) by road. Baklanovo is the nearest rural locality.
