Roman Vorobey (Ukrainian: Роман Валентинович Воробей; born 22 February 1994 in Chernihiv) is a Ukrainian football midfielder.


== Career ==
Vorobey is a product of his native Chernihiv's Yunist Chernihiv  youth sportive school system. The sports school is known for its talent players like Andriy Yarmolenko, Pavlo Polehenko, Serhiy Kovalenko, Vladyslav Shapoval, Pavlo Fedosov, Yuriy Maley, Oleksiy Khoblenko, Dimitry MironenkoAndriy Fedorenko,  and Nika Sichinava.He played in the different Ukrainian amateur and Second League clubs. In March 2015 Vorobey signed a contract with FC Skala and in July 2015 returned in the Belarusian Premier League club FC Slavia Mozyr.
