Montauriol (French pronunciation: [mɔ̃toʁjɔl] (listen); Catalan: Montoriol) is a commune in the Pyrénées-Orientales department in southern France.


== Geography ==
Montauriol is located in the canton of Les Aspres and in the arrondissement of Céret.
