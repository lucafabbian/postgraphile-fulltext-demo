While studying at the University of Cambridge, Lambert made two first-class appearances for Cambridge University Cricket Club, one in 1903 against Yorkshire and another against Worcestershire in 1904. Lambert made two further first-class appearances in 1904 for different teams, making an appearance for Sussex against Cambridge University at Hove, and for the Marylebone Cricket Club against London County Cricket Club at Crystal Palace. In his four first-class matches, he scored a total of 72 runs at an average of 12.00, with a high score of 30. With the ball, he took 3 wickets at a bowling average of 54.66, with best figures of 3/53.He died at Shaftesbury, Dorset, on 23 January 1968.
