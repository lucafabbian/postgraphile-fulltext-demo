Pawłowice [pavwɔˈvit͡sɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Mszczonów, within Żyrardów County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 15 kilometres (9 mi) south-east of Mszczonów, 26 km (16 mi) south-east of Żyrardów, and 49 km (30 mi) south-west of Warsaw.
