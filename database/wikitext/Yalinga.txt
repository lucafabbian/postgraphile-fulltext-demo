Yalinga is a town and sub-prefecture in the Haute-Kotto prefecture of the central-eastern Central African Republic.


== History ==
Yalinga was founded as a French post by Lieutenant Bissey on 1 March 1912 to anticipate any further attacks from Kamoun.On 28 February 2010, LRA attacked Yalinga. They pillaged police station, weather station, a safari camp and kidnapped 26 people.On 22 April 2021 Yalinga was captured from rebels of Coalition of Patriots for Change by Russian mercenaries allied with government forces.


== Transport ==
The town is served by Yalinga Airport.
