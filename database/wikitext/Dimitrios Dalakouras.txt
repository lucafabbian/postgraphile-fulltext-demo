Dimitrios Dalakouras (Greek: Δημήτριος Νταλακούρας; born 31 March 1999) is a Greek professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Panionios.


== Career ==


=== Panetolikos ===
Dalakouras began his career with the youth club of Panetolikos.
He made his Superleague debut on 3 February 2018 in a match against Panathinaikos.
