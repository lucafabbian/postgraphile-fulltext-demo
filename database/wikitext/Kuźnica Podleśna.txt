Kuźnica Podleśna (Polish pronunciation: [kuʑˈɲit͡sa pɔdˈlɛɕna]) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Siewierz, within Będzin County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 11 kilometres (7 mi) south of Siewierz, 8 km (5 mi) north-east of Będzin, and 20 km (12 mi) north-east of the regional capital Katowice.
The village has a population of 63.
