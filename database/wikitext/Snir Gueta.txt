Rabbi Snir Gueta (Hebrew: שניר גואטה, born 7 January 1988) is a retired Israeli professional football (soccer) player. 
He previously played for Maccabi Netanya, Hapoel Ashkelon, Maccabi Kiryat Malakhi, Hapoel Asi Gilboa and Maccabi Sha'arayim. At international level, Gueta was capped at under-17 and under-21 level.Following retirement, he became a rabbi and began lecturing on spiritual awakening, and is advocating for Jews to undergo a process of Tshuva.
