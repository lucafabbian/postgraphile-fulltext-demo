The New Nation Party is a registered political party in New Zealand. The party is led by Michael Jacomb and is a component party of Freedoms New Zealand. It opposes vaccine mandates, Three Waters, and the United Nations.


== History ==
The party stood former Tauranga City councillor Andrew Hollis as a candidate at the 2022 Tauranga by-election. He received 260 votes.The party applied for registration on 27 March 2023. It was registered on 12 April 2023.
