Albadi is a village in the Kundapur taluk of Udupi, Karnataka, India. 


== Demographics ==
In 2011, Albadi had a total population of 3,408 people, consisting of 1,846 males and 344 females. The village occupies a total area of 1640.00 hecatres with a population density of 2.078 people per hectare. In 2011, the literacy rate was 66.29%, which was higher than the national average of 59.5%.In 2011, Albadi had an employment rate of 47.42%, 95.36% of whom are employed over 183 days in a year.
