Xinghua Subdistrict (simplified Chinese: 兴华街道; traditional Chinese: 興華街道; pinyin: Xīnghuá Jiēdào) is a subdistrict and the seat of Zhalantun, Inner Mongolia, China. As of 2011, it has five residential communities (居委会) under its administration.
