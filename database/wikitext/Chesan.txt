Chesan (Russian: Чесан; Buryat: Чисаана / Шисаана, Chisaana / Shisaana) is a rural locality (a selo) in Kizhinginsky District, Republic of Buryatia, Russia. The population was 484 as of 2010. There are 10 streets.


== Geography ==
Chesan is located 54 km northeast of Kizhinga (the district's administrative centre) by road. Bulak is the nearest rural locality.
