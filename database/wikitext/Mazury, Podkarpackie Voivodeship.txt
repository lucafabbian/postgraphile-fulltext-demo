Mazury [maˈzurɨ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Raniżów, within Kolbuszowa County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) east of Raniżów, 22 km (14 mi) east of Kolbuszowa, and 26 km (16 mi) north of the regional capital Rzeszów.The village has a population of 1,010.
